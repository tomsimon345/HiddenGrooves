About Priya

Priya Nair is an award-winning classical dancer with over 15 years of training in Bharatanatyam and Kuchipudi. Trained under Guru Padmashree Saroja Vaidyanathan, she brings an authentic, emotionally rich performance to every stage. Priya has performed at the India International Centre, Nrityagram, and numerous cultural festivals across South India. Her unique fusion of classical vocabulary with contemporary narrative has earned her recognition across artistic communities.

She specializes in thematic solo performances, wedding sangeet choreography, and cultural evening showcases. Each performance is customized to the event's emotional and cultural context.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join as an artist?
Simply click "Join as an Artist" and create a free account. Set up your profile, upload a performance video, and you're live. You can upgrade to Pro or Premium at any time for greater visibility.
Is HiddenGrooves free to use?
Yes! Both artists and organizers can create free accounts. Paid subscriptions unlock premium features like priority search ranking, unlimited media uploads, and full booking tools.
How does the booking process work?
Organizers submit a booking request specifying event details, date, and budget. The artist reviews the request and accepts or declines within 24 hours. Once accepted, both parties communicate through the platform to finalize details.
What is the cancellation policy?
Cancellation policies vary by artist. Each artist profile displays their specific terms. We recommend confirming cancellation policies before finalizing a booking. Subscriptions can be cancelled anytime.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard under the Subscription tab.
